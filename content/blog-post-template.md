## Article Structure

### Introduction (150-200 words)
- Hook: Start with a problem or question
- Context: Why this topic matters
- Preview: What readers will learn
- Include primary keyword in first 100 words

**Example:**
```
Imagine a customer walks into your showroom with a photo of their dream 
tile on their phone, asking "Do you have something like this?" 

Traditional search methods fail here. Keyword search won't work because 
the customer doesn't know the style name, collection, or manufacturer. 
This is where AI-powered tile image search revolutionizes the industry.

In this guide, you'll discover how computer vision technology enables 
instant tile matching from photos, why leading tile manufacturers are 
adopting this technology, and how you can implement it in your business.
```

### Section 1: The Problem/Background (300-400 words)
- **H2**: The Challenge of Traditional Tile Discovery
- Explain current pain points
- Use statistics if available
- Include secondary keywords naturally

**Example Topics:**
- Why keyword search fails for design products
- Customer frustration with tile shopping
- Lost sales due to poor product discovery
- Industry statistics on search abandonment

### Section 2: The Solution (400-500 words)
- **H2**: How AI-Powered Visual Search Solves This Problem
- Explain the technology (computer vision)
- Benefits for businesses and customers
- Real-world use cases

**Example Topics:**
- What is visual search technology
- How computer vision analyzes images
- Pattern recognition and color matching
- Texture analysis and similarity scoring

### Section 3: Deep Dive/Technical Content (500-600 words)
- **H2**: The Technology Behind Visual Search
- **H3**: Image Recognition AI
- **H3**: Pattern Matching Algorithms
- **H3**: Color and Texture Analysis

**Include:**
- Screenshots or diagrams
- Step-by-step explanations
- Code examples (if technical audience)
- Comparison tables

### Section 4: Implementation/How-To (400-500 words)
- **H2**: How to Implement Visual Search in Your Tile Business
- Practical steps
- Tools and platforms
- Integration guide

**Example Steps:**
1. Assess your current product catalog
2. Choose a visual search platform (mention AxoVision)

4. Integrate with your e-commerce platform
5. Train your team
6. Monitor and optimize

- **H2**: The Business Impact of Visual Search
- ROI metrics
- Case studies (if available)
- Customer testimonials

**Include:**
- Percentage improvements
- Time savings
- Revenue increases
- Customer satisfaction scores
