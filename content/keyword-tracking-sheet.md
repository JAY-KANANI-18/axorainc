## Monthly Tracking Process

### Week 1 of Each Month:
1. Check rankings in Google Search Console
2. Update this tracker with current positions
3. Identify keywords moving up or down
4. Note any algorithm updates or changes

### Tracking Tools:
- **Google Search Console** (free, most accurate)
- **Ahrefs Rank Tracker** (paid, comprehensive)
- **SEMrush Position Tracking** (paid, good UI)
- **Manual Google Search** (use incognito mode)

### What to Track:
- **Average Position**: Where you rank on average
- **Impressions**: How many people see your result
- **Clicks**: How many people click through
- **CTR**: Click-through rate (clicks / impressions)

## Ranking Improvement Strategies

### If Keyword is Stuck:

**Rank 11-20 (Page 2):**
- Improve on-page SEO (title, H1, content)
- Add more depth to content
- Build 3-5 high-quality backlinks
- Update content freshness
- Improve user engagement signals

**Rank 21-50 (Page 3-5):**
- May need more content depth (longer article)
- Build more backlinks (10-20)
- Improve internal linking
- Check if search intent matches your content
- Consider keyword difficulty (may be too competitive)

**Rank 50+ or Not Ranking:**
- Content may not match search intent
- Keyword may be too competitive for current authority
- Technical SEO issues (check indexation)
- Need significantly more backlinks
- Consider targeting easier long-tail variations first
